Welcome to BookHive Reading App

BookHive is a free online platform for book lovers. With Reading App, you can:

  • Discover new books based on your reading history and preferences
  • Track the books you've read, the ones you're currently reading, and the ones you want to read
  • Connect with other book lovers through book clubs and social features
  • Customize your reading experience with customizable settings
  • Listen to audio books on the go
  • Create and organize your personal library of favorite books
